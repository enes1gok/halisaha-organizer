# Account and Data Deletion Request

Last updated: 2026-05-07  
Effective date: 2026-05-07

This page is provided for Google Play "Delete account URL" compliance and explains how users of Halisaha Organizer can request account and associated data deletion.

App/developer name: **Halisaha Organizer** (`Halısaha: Maç Organize Et` store listing)

## 1. How to request account deletion

To request deletion of your account and associated data:

1. Send an email to `privacy@halisaha.app`.
2. Use subject line: `Account Deletion Request`.
3. Include:
   - The account email used in the app
   - User id if available (as seen in profile context)
   - Request date and reachable contact email

Identity verification may be required for account security.

## 2. What data is deleted?

After request verification and where no legal exception applies, we delete or anonymize:

- Account record (authentication profile)
- Profile data (display name, photo URL, position, preferred foot, optional IBAN)
- Individual self-report related personal request records

## 3. What data may be retained?

The following categories may be retained for a limited period:

- Technical logs required for legal obligations or dispute handling
- Match/score history records required to preserve service/sports record integrity, with reduced direct personal linkage when applicable

Retention is limited to necessary scope and duration.

## 4. Timelines and retention periods

- Request response target: within 30 days (aligned with GDPR process expectations)
- Deletion completion target: typically within 30 days after identity verification
- Legal-retention exceptions: retained only for the period required by applicable law

## 5. In-app access

In app, use the `Hesabimi ve Verilerimi Sil` link at the bottom of the `Profil` tab.

## 6. Contact

For all privacy and deletion requests:

- Email: `privacy@halisaha.app`
