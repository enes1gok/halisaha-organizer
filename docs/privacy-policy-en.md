# Privacy Policy (GDPR-Oriented)

Last updated: 2026-05-07  
Effective date: 2026-05-07

This Privacy Policy explains how Halisaha Organizer ("App") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data.

## 1. Data Controller

Controller: Halisaha Organizer app operator  
Contact: `privacy@halisaha.app`

## 2. Categories of Personal Data

We may process:

- Account and identity data (email, user id, session data)
- Profile data (display name, photo URL, position, preferred foot, optional IBAN)
- Match participation data (RSVP, roster, team assignments, payment flags)
- Performance data (scores, goals, assists, match statistics, self-reports)
- Technical/local storage data (local cached app state on device)

## 3. Purposes and Legal Bases (GDPR Art. 6)

- **Contract performance** (Art. 6(1)(b)): account and match functionality
- **Legitimate interests** (Art. 6(1)(f)): service integrity, security, abuse prevention
- **Consent where applicable** (Art. 6(1)(a)): optional profile fields
- **Legal obligations** (Art. 6(1)(c)): mandatory legal disclosures/compliance

## 4. Sharing and Processors

Data may be shared with:

- Supabase as a processor (authentication and database infrastructure)
- Other users, where necessary for match organization features
- Competent authorities where legally required

We do not sell personal data.

## 5. International Transfers

Depending on infrastructure location and subprocessors, data may be transferred internationally with appropriate GDPR safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses, where applicable).

## 6. Retention

Data is kept for as long as needed for service purposes and legal obligations:

- Account/profile data: while account remains active
- Match/stat data: for service continuity and sports record integrity
- Local cache: until app uninstall, local data clearing, or migration reset

## 7. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect confidentiality, integrity, and availability of personal data.

## 8. Your GDPR Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request:

- Access
- Rectification
- Erasure
- Restriction
- Portability
- Objection (where processing relies on legitimate interest)
- Withdrawal of consent (where consent is the legal basis)

Please contact `privacy@halisaha.app`. We aim to respond within one month, subject to lawful extensions.

## 9. Children

The App is not intended for children below the applicable minimum legal age. If we become aware of improper collection, we will take reasonable deletion steps.

## 10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version date above governs.
